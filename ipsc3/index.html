Indore, the heart of Madhya Pradesh, is more than just a city — it's a vibrant blend of culture, cleanliness, commerce, and culinary richness. Recognized as India’s cleanest city for 7 consecutive years, Indore sets a benchmark in civic responsibility and smart urban development.

Cleanest City in India Indore’s success in the Swachh Survekshan rankings is no coincidence. With effective waste segregation, public engagement, and robust civic planning, it has become a national model for cleanliness and sustainability.

Commercial Capital of MP From bustling markets like Sarafa and Chappan Dukan to modern business hubs, Indore thrives as a trade and education center, attracting industries, startups, and investors from across India.

Foodie's Paradise Indore’s street food culture is iconic — blending North and Central Indian flavors with unique inventions. Night food markets, diverse vegetarian cuisine, and innovation make it a gastronomic landmark.
